Title: Berries Caipirinha

----

Text:

2 oz Cãna Classic Cachaça
0.5 oz simple syrup
10 raspberries

Glass - Rocks
Garnish - 3 Raspberries on a skewer

In a shaker, muddle raspberries and simple syrup. Add remaining ingredients and shake with ice. Strain into a rocks glass over fresh ice and garnish with skewered raspberries. 

Kitchen Hack: Substitute raspberries for whatever fresh berries you have on hand. Mix, match, and have fun!
